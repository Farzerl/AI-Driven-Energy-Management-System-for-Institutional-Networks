# Safety and control boundary

## Current stage

The Stage 3 evidence is monitoring-only. It simulates an edge gateway, validates readings, buffers data and sends it to the software MVP.

## Explicit exclusions

- no direct mains connection by the software team;
- no autonomous load switching;
- no control of critical loads;
- no bypass of existing protection or building-management systems;
- no claim of completed campus commissioning.

## Future supervised control requirements

Any later control interface must include site assessment, protection review, approved controllable-load schedules, electrical interlocks, manual override, fail-safe fallback to monitoring-only operation, communications-loss testing and institutional authorization.
